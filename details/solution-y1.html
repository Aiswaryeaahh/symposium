Solution A1:Zero-Emission Zones (ZEZs)

Planting Trees for a Greener Future

Establish Zero-Emission Zones in urban areas where only electric vehicles, bicycles, and pedestrians are permitted. By banning fossil fuel-powered transport, ZEZs reduce air pollution and carbon emissions while promoting clean mobility.
